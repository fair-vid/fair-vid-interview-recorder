## ğŸ¯ Purpose

This tool helps you create realistic mock video interview datasets that can be used to test and understand how AI systems analyze video interviews in university admissions or job applications. It's part of the FAIR-VID research project on transparent, human-centered AI in organizational processes.

### Why This Matters

Many universities and companies now use AI to analyze video interviews, but these systems are often "black boxes" - you can't see how they work. Our research aims to make AI in high-stakes decisions transparent and fair. This notebook lets **anyone** create test datasets to:

- Understand what AI "sees" in video interviews
- Test AI systems on your own computer
- Practice answering interview questions
- Help researchers build better, fairer AI systems
